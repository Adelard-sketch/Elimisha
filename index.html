Who We Are

A center built on community and purpose

Founded in the heart of our community, Elimisha Education Center — elimisha meaning "to educate" in Swahili — was born from a simple belief: every child deserves a world-class education, regardless of background.

We offer holistic programs that address academic excellence, emotional growth, and practical life skills.

  • 🌱 Holistic Development
  • 🤝 Community-Led
